Goals & Objectives

Project Goals

Create an Intuitive and User-Centric Design

Develop a simple and clear interface that prioritizes ease of navigation.

Reduce onboarding complexity to create a welcoming experience for new users.

Solve Key User Pain Points

Provide a streamlined budget-tracking feature that feels faster and easier than pen and paper.

Offer real-time insights into spending without overwhelming users with unnecessary complexity.

Ensure Accessibility and User Attraction

Launch with a free version to reduce barriers to entry and encourage adoption.

Focus on building trust by avoiding ads or unnecessary paywalls in the initial release.

Foster Engagement and Retention

Gather user feedback regularly to guide updates and feature development.

Create a seamless experience that encourages daily interaction and long-term retention.

Lay the Groundwork for Scalability

Plan for premium features (e.g., subscription or advanced tools) based on user demand and feedback.

Align future updates with user needs and market trends.

Project Objectives

Design and Development Objectives

Finalize the core design, ensuring simplicity and user-friendliness.

Complete key feature development.

User Testing and Feedback Objectives

Conduct usability testing with participants to validate ease of use.

Gather and analyze beta feedback post-launch to identify improvement areas.

Marketing and Launch Objectives

Develop a marketing campaign highlighting the app's simplicity and user-centric features.

Aim for downloads in the first three months post-launch, adjusting based on user acquisition strategies.

Monetization and Growth Objectives

Define premium feature ideas and rollout strategies based on early user engagement metrics.

Set achievable user retention goals to gauge app success.

Feedback and Improvement Objectives

Integrate an in-app feedback mechanism to collect real-time user suggestions.

Review and act on feedback bi-weekly, focusing on the most requested updates and bug fixes.
