Design Approach

The e-book page design emphasizes readability and visual appeal through careful typography selection, strategic use of white space, and thoughtful placement of visual elements. The design incorporates the 80/20 principle not only in content but also in visual hierarchy, ensuring that the most important information captures 80% of the reader's attention.

Key Design Elements:

  • Typography Hierarchy: Clear distinction between chapter title, body text, and tip box
  • Visual Elements: Strategic use of graphics to support the learning concept
  • Layout Structure: A4 vertical format optimized for digital and print reading
  • Brand Consistency: Aligned with BrainyJuice brand guidelines and educational focus
