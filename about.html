Get to Know Me

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer with a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences

A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Ghaleb Saidi, a soon-to-be Computer Science graduate with a passion for creating intuitive and impactful user experiences. My journey into the world of UI/UX design combines my love for technology, problem-solving, and artistic expression.

From a young age, I was drawn to design and art, constantly creating and expressing myself visually. As I delved into computer science, I realized the incredible synergy between art and technology. This led me to UI/UX design, where I could merge creativity with functionality to craft solutions that make users' lives easier and more enjoyable.

I believe in designing experiences that are not only visually appealing but also solve real-world problems. My mindset revolves around finding opportunities in challenges, guided by the belief that "where there is a problem, there is profit." I thrive on innovation and creating from scratch, whether it's designing interfaces or developing solutions through code.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me immersed in bodybuilding and calisthenics, playing instruments like the guitar and piano, or diving into books about business and psychology. I'm constantly learning and exploring new skills to fuel my creativity and growth.

My Vision

As I approach graduation in July 2025, my goal is to join a team of experienced designers where I can learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully. I believe in the power of collaboration and value the opportunity to work alongside brilliant minds who can guide me to reach my full potential.

Ultimately, I aspire to create innovative solutions that not only meet users' needs but inspire them as well. I aim to build products that are both beautiful and functional, focusing on accessibility and inclusivity in all my designs.

I'm passionate about continuous learning and staying ahead of industry trends, which I believe is essential in the ever-evolving field of UI/UX design and web development.
